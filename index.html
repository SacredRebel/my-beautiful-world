Made by a father, for his daughter

I couldn't find it anywhere. So I created it myself.

My little girl is growing up between languages — the ones her family speaks, the ones her roots carry.

I wanted her first book to hold all of them at once.

Not an app. Not flashcards that beep. Something warm she could hold in her lap.

I searched. That book didn't exist.

So over many months I made it — page by page. A lion. Our kitchen. Our dog, Leo. The night sky.

Every thing in every picture, named in six languages — each language in its own color, so she could learn the whole world by name.

This is that book. I made it for her. Now I'm sharing it with your family too.

— from one parent to another
